# Michael Burry — Scion Asset Management Q3 2025 13F-HR

## Summary

Burry's Q3 2025 filing (Nov 3, 2025) shows an extremely concentrated 8-position portfolio worth ~$1.38B. Palantir (PLTR) dominates at 66% of the book — a massive bet on defense/AI government contracting. NVIDIA (NVDA) at 14% and Pfizer (PFE) at 11% round out the top three. ## Key Claims

1. **Burry is all-in on Palantir.** 66% of portfolio in a single name is extreme even for Burry. PLTR is a defense/government AI play — not pure tech, more defense contractor. This could be a Gulf War III catalyst play if the position was built with geopolitical escalation in mind.

2. **Energy exposure is minimal — just HAL.** Only Halliburton at 4.5%, an oilfield services name (not E&P or integrated). No CVX, OXY, DVN, or other direct energy producers. Burry is playing energy through the services picks-and-shovels angle.

3. **PFE is classic Burry contrarian value.** Pfizer has been beaten down post-COVID — 6M shares at $25.48 avg. This is a reversion play on a historically reliable dividend payer trading at multi-year lows.

4. **NVDA shows Burry isn't bearish on AI infrastructure.** 1M shares of NVIDIA ($186M) alongside the PLTR position suggests Burry sees the AI buildout as real — he's just playing it through defense (PLTR) and hardware (NVDA) rather than hyperscalers.
