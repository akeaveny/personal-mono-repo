### Car
- This is a **depreciating asset** — minimize the drag on net worth
- AutoTrader: buy used, 2-3 years old, let someone else eat the depreciation
- Car from dad: if this is free/cheap, take it and invest what you would've spent
- Keep total car cost (purchase + insurance + gas + maintenance) under ___% of income

### Employer Equity (HELIOS)
- Understand the terms: vesting schedule, exercise price, liquidity events
- If pre-IPO: this is a lottery ticket, not a plan. Don't count it in net worth until it's liquid
- If public/liquid: decide hold vs sell based on concentration risk (don't let one stock be >10% of net worth)
