# VFV — Vanguard S&P 500 Index ETF (June 2025)

## Summary

ETF Facts sheet for VFV, the Vanguard S&P 500 Index ETF listed on the TSX in CAD. This is a core US equity holding providing broad large-cap US exposure. 505 holdings, 0.09% MER, $20.6B AUM. The fund is heavily weighted toward IT (30.4%) with top 10 holdings at 35.6%, dominated by mega-cap tech.

## Key Claims

1. **IT is 30.4% of VFV.** The single largest sector weight. Combined with Communication Services (9.3%) and Consumer Discretionary (10.4%, which includes Amazon and Tesla), tech-adjacent exposure is ~50% of the fund.
2. **Top 10 = 35.6%.** Apple 6.8%, Microsoft 6.2%, NVIDIA 5.6%, Amazon 3.7%, Alphabet 3.6%, Meta 2.5%, Berkshire 2.1%, Broadcom 1.9%, Tesla 1.7%, Eli Lilly 1.5%. Concentration risk in mega-cap tech is significant.
3. **Energy at only 3.2%.** For a thesis that overweights energy, VFV provides almost no energy exposure. This is the opposite of XIC/XDIV's problem — VFV underweights energy relative to the thesis.
4. **MER 0.09%.** Extremely low cost, identical to XIC. Appropriate as a core holding.
5. **2024 return 35.24%, since-inception CAGR 16.25%.** Strong long-term performance from US large-cap exposure. Only 1 negative year in the last 10 (2022: -12.69%).

## Thesis Impact

- **Impact:** NEUTRAL
- **Tickers affected:** VFV.TO (core holding)
- **Claims touched:**
  - *NEUTRAL* — Core portfolio strategy. VFV serves the same role as VTI in the thesis — broad US equity exposure. No changes needed to the core allocation.
  - *SUPPORTS* — "Big Tech/Cloud: Neutral, already well-represented in core ETF holdings." With IT at 30.4% and the Mag 7 dominating the top 10, owning VFV already provides heavy tech exposure. No need to double up in satellite positions.
  - *SUPPORTS* — Energy satellite thesis. VFV's 3.2% energy weight means any energy satellite positions (CVX, LNG, VLO) are genuinely additive, not redundant with the core.

## What I'm Taking Away

- VFV is the US equity core holding. At 0.09% MER and 505 holdings, there's no reason to change it.
- The 30.4% IT weight means the AI/semis watchlist names (NVDA, AVGO, MRVL) already have partial exposure through VFV. This reinforces the thesis decision to wait for a pullback before adding satellite AI/semi positions — you're already partially exposed through the core.
- Energy at 3.2% confirms that satellite energy positions are additive and meaningful against a core with minimal energy weighting.
