# PLTR — Equity Research Memo (April 2026)

## Summary

A 20+ page deep-dive equity memo on Palantir Technologies dated April 16, 2026. Rates PLTR as HOLD / WAIT-FOR-ENTRY with a quality score of 96/100 but concludes the valuation (47.2x EV/Revenue, ~200x P/E TTM) leaves no margin of safety. The skew gate fails — 28% expected upside vs 43.7% bear-case downside. The core thesis: PLTR is a world-class business trading at a price that requires flawless execution and no competitive disruption.

## Key Claims

1. **Ontology moat is real but potentially temporary.** Palantir's proprietary semantic layer ("Ontology") maps an organization's entire physical and digital state into a digital twin. Switching costs are massive — removing Palantir requires rebuilding the entire semantic decision-making framework. However, if Anthropic's Claude Code or OpenAI agents can autonomously map legacy data schemas into functional logic without manual ontology building, the moat erodes. Probability of this "Agentic Bypass" by late 2027 estimated at 35%.

2. **US Commercial growth is explosive: 137% YoY in Q4 2025.** Revenue guidance of $7.18-$7.20B for FY2026 (+61% YoY). AIP Bootcamps compress sales cycles from 6-9 months to days, achieving ~75% conversion to multi-year contracts. Net dollar retention at 139%. Rule of 40 score: 127%.

3. **Financial profile is fortress-like.** Zero debt, $7.17B cash + securities, projected $3.92-$4.12B FCF in 2026 (~55% FCF margin). Gross margins 82.4%. SBC declining as % of revenue (15.3% in 2025 vs 24% in 2024) but still $684M absolute.

4. **Insider selling is heavy and concerning.** $580M in planned insider sales under 10b5-1 plans. Peter Thiel sold $280M, Alex Karp sold $66M (Feb-March 2026). No meaningful open-market purchases by directors at $200B+ market cap. Management appears to view stock as "fairly valued to fully valued."

5. **Valuation trades at 440% premium to peers.** PLTR at 47.2x EV/Revenue vs SNOW 8.2x, MSFT 8.7x. Reverse DCF implies market expects 32.8% FCF CAGR for 10 years. Fair value band: $115 (bear), $190 (base), $260 (bull).

6. **Private credit stress is a hidden macro risk.** Fitch reports 5.8% trailing-12-month private credit default rate (highest ever). ~26% of direct lending exposed to software borrowers. Redemption gates hit marquee funds (Ares, Apollo, Blue Owl, BlackRock HPS). PIK interest now 11% of private loans.
