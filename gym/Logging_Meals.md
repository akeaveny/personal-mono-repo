## How to Use

1. Dump raw notes here (what you ate, how you felt, why you ate it)
2. Run `/log-meal` — Claude estimates macros, flags triggers, writes the daily summary
3. Transfer the **Daily Total** row into your Excel nutrition tab
4. At end of week, review triggers and update `Meal_Prep.md` if needed
