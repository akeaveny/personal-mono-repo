## Buffett's Cash Position (Context)

Not in the 13F (equities only), but critical context from Berkshire's Q4 2025 earnings:
- Cash + T-bills: **~$320–334B** (record high)
- Equity portfolio: ~$280–300B
- **~50% of investable capital is in cash/T-bills**

Buffett has been a net seller of equities for 8+ straight quarters. His implicit message: nothing is cheap enough to deploy at scale. This is the most important macro signal in the entire filing.
